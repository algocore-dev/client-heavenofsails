What happens next

Your project in 5 phases

1

Week 1–2

Discovery & architecture

Wireframes, user flows, and database schema. You review and approve before we move on. Changes here cost zero.
2

Week 2–4

UI/UX design

Full interactive Figma prototype. Click through every screen before a single line is coded. Two revision rounds included.
3

Week 4–9

Development — the quiet phase

Deep coding happens here. You will receive weekly written updates every Tuesday but may go 2–3 weeks without visible output. This is normal.
4

Week 9–11

QA & staging

You receive a private staging link. Test on mobile and desktop, log any bugs — we handle the rest.
5

Week 11–12

Launch & handoff

Push to production, full credential handoff, and a final walkthrough call together. 30-day post-launch support included.
